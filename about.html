"Product Innovator"

Yidan Xu

I'm a London-based product designer and innovation researcher, with a double master's in Innovation Design Engineering from Imperial College London and the Royal College of Art (2025). My work operates at the intersection of people, science, and design.

Her individual work has received international recognition, including being named a UK James Dyson Award runner-up and global top 20, and being featured by Dezeen and Reuters. My current venture, Urify, is being incubated at Imperial College London's We Innovate programme. I lead its cross-functional medical and engineering team as solo founder, and have presented it to the NHS and at TEDx.

I'm always open to cross-disciplinary collaborations and chances to push my work further. I've also contributed to projects at Huawei and Xiaomi Auto, applying a human-centred design approach to complex product systems.

Timeline

Wide-ranging exploration, one design sensibility.

Rather than staying in one lane, I've deliberately moved across industries: health tech, dating, automotive, hardware, branding. Each one gave me a different way of thinking, and I carry it into the next.

My résumé doesn't read like a ladder. It reads like a network. Six industries in three years isn't job-hopping; it's how my generation actually works: pick up an unfamiliar domain fast, treat AI as native to the process rather than bolted onto it, and carry what each stop taught into the next one.
