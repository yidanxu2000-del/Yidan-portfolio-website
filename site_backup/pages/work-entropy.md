## Project Overview
The game is set 50 years in the future in the West Westminster area. West Westminster is the place with the lowest recycling rate and the most serious environmental problems in the whole of London, and it is also the homeless center of London and has a great risk of flooding.

Our game envisages that 50 years later, the flood carries garbage back to human society, and how we should deal with it.
