### RCA Team Work Story Board
This storyboard, illustrated by me with the assistance of AI tools, captures a dystopian future in a flooded Westminster. It follows a homeless character navigating through rain-soaked, trash-filled streets, exploring themes of survival and hope in a decaying city.

### RCA Team Work Scene Design
These scenes are designed to represent a future flooded urban environment, filled with waste and pollution. The protagonist trudges through the desolate waters, exploring the lost city and trying to restore balance to the environment. Through light, composition, and character interaction, each scene creates the heaviness of apocalyptic ruins and the glimmer of hope.
