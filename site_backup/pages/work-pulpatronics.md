## Background
This color palette gives a slightly aged and muted feel due to its desaturated tones and earthy undertones. The dark blues, browns, and greys lack vibrancy, making them feel subdued and somewhat dusty. The beige and off-white shades further enhance this "worn" look, as they resemble aged paper or faded materials.

Secondly, without a contrasting accent, there's no focal point to draw attention, reducing the overall impact and visual appeal. Adding a brighter or more saturated highlight color could help create contrast and improve clarity.

This is the old color pattern. 🔒 For confidentiality reasons.

## Color upgrade strategy
**Introduce A Highlight Color** — Bring contrast and energy, making the system more engaging.

**Redefine Neutrals** — Adjusting beige and off-white to be cleaner and fresher make a more modern and professional look.

**Balance Reliable & Innovate** — Keeping the deep navy for trustworthiness while adding brighter elements to reflect a rising startup.

**Align With Sustainability** — Improve a blue-green accent reinforces the brand's eco-friendly mission and innovation in sustainable tech.

Added a Highlight Color – A vibrant turquoise brings contrast and energy, making the design more engaging.
Refined Neutral Tones – Cleaner beige and off-white create a more polished and modern look.
Improved Balance – Deep navy and muted blues maintain trustworthiness, while the turquoise adds freshness and innovation.
Stronger Alignment with Sustainability – The blue-green accent reinforces the company's eco-friendly and startup-driven identity.
